Who is Jesse Barkdoll?

He is a designer and aspiring front-end developer currently studying and coding in JavaScript. As a self-proclaimed maker, he has always had a passion to create. Coming from a design background, he seems to be working toward what people in tech call— a unicorn. His goal is to learn full-stack development and utilize his design skills to be visionary in the web/application development process.

Aside from design, he is a language learner, musician, and occasional blogger. He has been studying the Japanese language seriously since 2015. If you'd like to read about his experiences in Japanese, tech, art, film, etc., you can check out his blog here.

Location: Baltimore, MD
Currently: Seeking a web design/development job in the creative tech industry

誰がバックドール・ジェシー？！ (日本語版)

皆さんようこそ。デザイナーであり、プログラマーを目指してJavaScriptを勉強しているジェシーと申します。僕はずっとクリエイティブなことに対して情熱を持ってきた根っからのクリエイターです。目標はプログラミングとデザインの技術を融合させた素敵なwebプロジェクトに携わることです。

デザイン以外にも、音楽家であり、時折ライターでもあります。加えて、僕は日本語を3年以上勉強しています。これらの経験についてブログで発信していくので、興味がある方は是非チェックしてみてください！
